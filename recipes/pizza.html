Pizza

Description

Pizza is a dish of Italian origin consisting of a usually round, flat base of leavened wheat-based dough topped with tomatoes, cheese, and often various other ingredients, which is then baked at a high temperature, traditionally in a wood-fired oven. A small pizza is sometimes called a pizzetta.

Ingredients

  • Pancetta
  • Spaghetti
  • Black Pepper
  • Parmesan
  • Egg Yolk
  • Peas (optional)

Steps

  1. Dice pancetta
  2. Fry pancetta in oil until cripsy
  3. Seperate egg yolks
  4. Mix parmesan into egg yolks
  5. Cook pasta in salted boiling water until al dente
  6. Add pasta to frying pan with pancetta
  7. Gradually pour yolk mix and stir into pasta
  8. Season to taste with black pepper
