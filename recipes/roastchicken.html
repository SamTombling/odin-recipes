Roast Chicken

Description

There's nothing better than a delicious Sunday roast. Follow this Jamie Oliver roast chicken recipe for beautifully succulent results. Just add spuds!

Ingredients

  • Pancetta
  • Spaghetti
  • Black Pepper
  • Parmesan
  • Egg Yolk
  • Peas (optional)

Steps

  1. Dice pancetta
  2. Fry pancetta in oil until cripsy
  3. Seperate egg yolks
  4. Mix parmesan into egg yolks
  5. Cook pasta in salted boiling water until al dente
  6. Add pasta to frying pan with pancetta
  7. Gradually pour yolk mix and stir into pasta
  8. Season to taste with black pepper
