Carbonara

Description

Carbonara is an Italian pasta dish from Rome made with eggs, hard cheese, cured pork, and black pepper. The dish arrived at its modern form, with its current name, in the middle of the 20th century.

Ingredients

  • Pancetta
  • Spaghetti
  • Black Pepper
  • Parmesan
  • Egg Yolk
  • Peas (optional)

Steps

  1. Dice pancetta
  2. Fry pancetta in oil until cripsy
  3. Seperate egg yolks
  4. Mix parmesan into egg yolks
  5. Cook pasta in salted boiling water until al dente
  6. Add pasta to frying pan with pancetta
  7. Gradually pour yolk mix and stir into pasta
  8. Season to taste with black pepper
